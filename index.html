About Me

I am a 4th year PhD candidate at Université Laval supervised by Jean-François Lalonde and Yannick Geoffroy-Hold.

I have always loved to create. I create to spark the imagination. To color the mind. To fascinate. I picture a world in which everyone see the enchantment of life when opening the eyes to the immense creative potential around us. Being a Ph.D. candidate in electrical engineering at Laval University and specializing in computer vision, I stand out with my technical abilities and my creative mind.
